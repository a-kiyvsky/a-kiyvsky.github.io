About Us

The Dominion KX III is Raritan's flagship, enterprise-class KVM-over-IP switch that provides 1, 2, 4 or 8 users with BIOS-level, remote management of 8, 16, 32 or 64 servers in a single switch. With industry leading video performance, security, and reliability, the Dominion KX III outperforms the competition.

With standard features such as DVI/HDMI/DisplayPort digital video, VGA analog video, virtual media, audio, smart card/CAC, and mobile access, the Dominion KX III is suitable for both general computer and dynamic broadcast applications.
